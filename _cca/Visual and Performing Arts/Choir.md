#### **Choir**


![](/images/06%20CCA/VPA%20Choir/Picture14C.jpg)
Post-performance at the commemoration ceremony for the 80th Anniversary of the End of World War II held at Kranji War Cemetery on Sept 12, 2025.&nbsp;<br>Front row (L-R): Teachers Mrs Fung and Ms Tay, Principal of CHIJ St Nicholas Girls’ School Winnie Teo, former SAF Chief of Defence Force LG (Ret) Winston Choo and his spouse Katherine Choo, Director of Singapore History consultants Jeya Ayadurai.
<br><br>The **CHIJ St. Nicholas Girls’ Secondary Choir (SN Choir)** comprises passionate choristers aged 13 to 16 who take immense pride in representing their school in performances and competitions.

United by our love for music, SN Choir members are motivated not only by the pursuit of musical excellence but also by the strong friendships forged through singing together. Over the years, we have performed at numerous concerts, competitions, and community events, sharing the joy of choral music with wider audiences.

Guided by our dedicated conductor and supportive teachers, we continually strive to grow through meaningful and varied musical experiences.

**We are SN Choir — and we create the dream.**
